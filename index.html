Synapse Simulator

A demonstration of the concept of a neuronal connection via a chemical synapse. Input neurons will fire at varying rates to release neurotransmitters that when bound to the receptors of the secondary neuron, increase the voltage of the neuron until its action potential is reached. At this point, the secondary neuron will fire. Neurons have a refractory period after firing is complete.

Use arrow keys to change view.
